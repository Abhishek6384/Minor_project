Welcome To Mask Detection...!!!

In the new world of COVID-19 pandemic, multidisciplinary efforts have been organized to slow the spread of the pandemic. The AI community has also been a part of these endeavours. In particular, developments for monitoring social distancing or identifying face masks have made the headlines.

Application to check your Employee for Mask.

The project is about to identify whether the person working in an organization or company is wearing a mask or not. If the person is not wearing a mask he or she is not allowed to enter in the organization.

The following are the benefits of this project:
  • Reduces the chance of transmission of virus.
  • No third person needed for checking the mask.
